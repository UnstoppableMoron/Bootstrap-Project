Pokémon Emerald was released in Japan in 2004, and in 2005 internationally as an expanded release of Pokémon Ruby and Sapphire, and the final game of generation 3 of Pokémon. It is one of the most praised Pokémon games, even twenty years later.
The Pokémon games and the species designs were not made by me, but instead by designers at Game Freak.
